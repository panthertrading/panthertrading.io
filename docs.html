What signals mean

STRONG BUY
High alignment + healthier structure. Still manage risk.
WAIT / WATCH
Early or unclear. Wait for confirmation.
DON’T INVEST
Structural risk detected. Avoid chasing.
DEGEN BUY
High risk / high variance. Only for aggressive traders.

The goal is to avoid bad trades first — then catch the good ones.

Confidence & phase

Confidence is how strongly the data supports the current signal.

Phase explains where the token is in its lifecycle (early discovery, momentum, distribution, etc.).

Risk indicators (what they mean)

Indicator What it usually implies What you should do
Rug Risk Liquidity / ownership patterns that often precede rugs. Reduce exposure or avoid completely.
Bundler Risk Wallet clustering / patterns suggesting manipulation. Be cautious with entries; watch exits closely.
Whale Risk Large holders can dump hard and fast. Prefer small sizing, tighter exits.

Note: No tool is perfect. Panther reduces risk — it does not eliminate it.
